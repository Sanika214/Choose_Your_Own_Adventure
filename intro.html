Intro

You are Detective , a young investigator who is known for your keen eye and persistence. You have been called to solve the case of a missing high school student, Chloe Smith. Chloe, a smart and friendly cheerleader, was last seen on Wednesday, 2 days ago by her friends and family. As Chloe’s sudden disappearance is out of character, you begin in Chloe’s room to begin your investigation.

Your mission is to gather clues, follow leads and uncover what happened. But remember, each choice you make affects where this investigation goes. Be careful, and let's find Chloe!
