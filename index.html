Welcome to Unsolved!

A Choose-Your-Own-Adventure Game!

In Unsolved, every choice you make will impact the outcome of the story. As a detective tasked with uncovering the mystery of a missing high school student, Chloe Smith, you’ll need to gather clues, make tough decisions, and follow leads carefully to solve the case.

Throughout the game, you’ll be asked what you’d like to do next; each decision could move you forward or bring the investigation to an unexpected end.

Remember to type in your answer in ALL CAPS and to type “STOP” at any point to exit the game. If you are stuck on a decision, hover over the corresponding icons for a hint on potential outcomes!

Good luck, Detective, and remember: Chloe’s fate rests in your hands.
