Help

Welcome to the help menu. Here’s what you need to know:

  • Type your answer in ALL CAPS
  • Type "STOP" at any time to quit the game.
  • Hover over the icons next to decisions if you are unsure.
  • Read each choice carefully — your decisions will affect the outcome.
  • If your response isn’t working, double-check that you've entered it correctly.
  • If you reach a “Game Over,” you can restart (click on Intro button on menu bar) to try a different path.
  • Remember to think like a detective and pay attention to details!

Good Luck!
