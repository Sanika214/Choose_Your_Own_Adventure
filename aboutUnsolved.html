About Unsolved

Unsolved is an interactive story game created by Sanika Chakkamadathil, Anahat Ghuman, and Lindsey Yu for the FBLA competitive event, Introduction to Programming. The game allows players to make decisions that impact the storyline and lead to different outcomes. Players can stop interacting at any time by typing "stop."

Thank you for playing our game!
